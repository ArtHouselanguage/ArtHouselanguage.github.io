About us

Welcome to Art House Language Center!!!

Since 2009 we have been teaching foreign languages to different age groups. We develop new interactive methods trying to provide basic knowledge as easy and quickly as possible. We conduct classes from speaking to technical and professional level and we do preparation and support for various international exams as required in short terms.


Whether you choose an individual or group variant you will receive a special approach from our high qualified specialists who will satisfy even the most stingy student with their work experience and knowledge and the result will be the key to your success. Knowing foreign languages multiplies the opportunities and success, ensures a professional rises.


Become competitive in any field by improving your foreign languages skills at Art House Language Center!!!
