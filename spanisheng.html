Language teaching is also an art...


Spanish is considered a state language in more than twenty countries of the world. It is estimated that the total number of speakers of Spanish is between four hundred and seventy to five hundred million, which means that it is one of the 2nd most widely spoken languages. According to 2007 data, Spanish is the 3rd most popular language by the total number of speakers after Chinese and English.

The Art House Learning Center offers accelerated courses in spoken Spanish, an excellent command of the language, which today is considered the key to success. We also organize professional Spanish language courses.


Classes are conducted by high-class specialists using the latest methodology, 3 classes per week lasting 90 minutes. The first lesson is free.

  • Professional terminology, phrases, dialogues in Spanish and Armenian versions
  • Watching and discussing short films
  • Study of literature, grammar through exercises, tasks, translations
  • Active discussions, reproduction of texts in Spanish
  • Speaking and writing development for applicants
  • Intensive speaking courses
